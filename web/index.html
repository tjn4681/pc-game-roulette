Welcome to PC Game Roulette

Can't decide what to play? This app spins a random game from your library. Your Steam, GOG, Epic, and RetroArch libraries are detected automatically — pick a tab and spin.

One quick optional step for Steam

By default the app sees your installed Steam games and any you've sorted into Steam Collections.

Already organize your games into Steam Collections? You're all set — skip this. Your collections already give the app your games.

Don't use Collections? To spin across your entire owned library — including games you haven't installed — paste a free Steam Web API key below.
Get a free key in ~30s → steamcommunity.com/dev/apikey
Sign in, enter any domain (e.g. localhost), agree, and copy the key. Tip: you can right-click the box → Paste.
You can add or change this any time in Settings → Full Steam Library.
